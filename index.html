About

Rollin Hu is a producer for The Ezra Klein Show at The New York Times, where he researches, reports, and produces episodes on politics, policy, technology, and culture.

Before joining the Times, he worked at The Atlantic, The New Republic, and WYPR, Baltimore's NPR station. His writing has appeared in The Nation, Baltimore Beat, and The Johns Hopkins News-Letter, where he served as Editor-in-Chief.

He studied history and economics at Johns Hopkins University, with a focus on 20th-century China.
