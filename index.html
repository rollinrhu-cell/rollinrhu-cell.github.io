About

Rollin Hu is a producer for The Ezra Klein Show at The New York Times, where he reports on economics, politics, and technology.

Before joining the Times, he worked at The Atlantic, The New Republic, and WYPR, Baltimore's NPR station. His writing has also appeared in The Nation.
